🇨🇿 Stock Tax Calculator

Calculate taxable amounts for E-Trade RSU and ESPP transactions according to Czech tax law.

Select which plan types to include in calculations

At Work → My Account → Benefit History → Download Expanded

At Work → My Account → Gains & Losses → Pick Year → Download Expanded

Yearly Average Rates (GFŘ "jednotný kurz"):
2019: 22.93 | 2020: 23.14 | 2021: 21.72 | 2022: 23.41 | 2023: 22.14 | 2024: 23.28 | 2025: 21.84
Daily rates are fetched from CNB for all years.

ℹ️ How to export from E-Trade

  • Benefit History (BenefitHistory.xlsx):
    At Work → My Account → Benefit History → Download Expanded
    (Downloads Excel with "ESPP" and "Restricted Stock" sheets)
  • Gains & Losses (G&L_Expanded.xlsx):
    At Work → My Account → Gains & Losses → Pick Year (e.g., 2025) → Download Expanded
    (Contains both RSU and ESPP sell transactions)

✅ Just upload the Excel files directly - no conversion needed!
